Troposphere

This is the lowest layer where all weather happens. Contains clouds and supports most life on Earth.

Stratosphere

The ozone layer is located here, absorbing ultraviolet radiation and helping to regulate temperature.

Mesosphere

Meteors burn up in this layer, and temperatures decrease as altitude increases.

Thermosphere

This layer absorbs high-energy radiation, creating phenomena like the auroras (Northern and Southern Lights).
